Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

FromSoftware, Hidetaka Miyazaki, 2019

"Hard Core"


"Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice is the innovation from Dark Souls, the well-known franchise of FromSoftware. It brings the breathtaking combat, amazing map design and gorgeous musics. It is true that this game is very hard, and it does not fit everyone. But for people who like challenge, they shall not miss that."

IGN: 9.5/GameSpot: 9/Metascore: 8.8.

Verdict


Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice wins the Game of the Year 2019. The gameplay is exciting and emergent, and worth challenging. In the meantime, Sekiro has one of the best soundtrack of this year. It is the piece that exceeds the cultural things behind itself .

Playability: 9/Graphic: 7/Narrative: 7/Music: 9
